INSTRUCTIONS

  • Drag and drop instruments onto the stage to start playing that instrument's part of the song.
  • To remove an instument from the stage and stop playing that instrument, click on the icon on the stage.
  • To remove all instruments from the stage and stop the music completely, click on the reset button.
  • For the best experience, drag instruments of the same genre on stage to play a song!
  • If an instrument doesn't play right away, don't worry, it just isn't playing in that part of the song!
